Muda Complete Primary School

The construction of the EPC Muda Mufo is an integral part of the reconstruction and recovery efforts following the devastation caused by Cyclone Idai in March 2019. The cyclone significantly damaged many infrastructures in the region, including schools. This new school building is designed not only to replace the destroyed structures but also to enhance the quality of educational facilities, ensuring that the community has access to a safe and adequate learning environment. This project entails the expansion of the existing Muda Mufo Primary School (EPC), located on a relatively flat site with an area of approximately 29,252.48 m² and a built-up area of 1,942.9 m². The construction includes three blocks, covering civil, hydraulic, electrical, and external works as outlined in the project drawings.

See about the project

Tica Complete Primary School

This project involves the expansion of the Tica Secondary School (ESG), located on a relatively flat plot of approximately 44,310.4006 m². The new building, which will cover an area of 1,795.53 m², represents an extension of the existing school. The structure under construction includes a block with 11 classrooms, designed to accommodate the growing student population. In addition to the civil engineering and landscaping works detailed in the drawings, the project also includes the reconstruction of the pavement of a multipurpose court. This court was removed to make way for the construction of the new classroom building. Once completed, the school will have a total of 22 classrooms, with a capacity to serve 1,150 students per shift.
