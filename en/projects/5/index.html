Project Planning

After identifying the need, a detailed plan was created for the establishment of the school. This planning involved clearly defining the institution's mission and vision, which focused on providing children with a solid educational foundation and ethical values. Additionally, studies were conducted to determine the number of classes, the curriculum to be followed, the necessary teaching resources, and the ideal physical structure to house school activities. The plan also included the development of a timeline for construction and the gradual implementation of activities.
